_USB_HUB_CAPABILITIES_EX structure

Description

The USB_HUB_CAPABILITIES_EX structure is used with the IOCTLUSB_GET_HUBCAPABILITIES I/O control request to retrieve the capabilities of a particular USB hub.

Members

CapabilityFlags

A USB_HUB_CAP_FLAGS structure that reports the hub capabilities.
